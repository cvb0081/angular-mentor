# SETUP — first-run interview

> **Scaffold stub.** Instructions for the AI to onboard a new learner.

When a learner starts, run a short interview to fill `CONFIG.md`, then seed their roadmap.

## Interview (one question at a time)

1. What language/framework do you want to learn?
2. What's your background — where are you already strong, where weak?
3. What's your goal / desired outcome?
4. Any scope limits (e.g. frontend-only, no backend)?
5. A capstone app idea, or should the AI propose one?
6. Mentor preferences (hand-holding level, pace)?

## After the interview

1. Write the answers into `CONFIG.md`.
2. Research/assemble a milestone roadmap for the chosen stack → `ROADMAP.md`.
3. Seed `PROGRESS.md` and `FRESHNESS.md`.
4. Start milestone 1's diagnostic quiz.
